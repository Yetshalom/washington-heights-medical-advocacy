How to share information safely

You control what you share. We aim to collect only what is needed to help with next steps.

Good to share

  • Your name and preferred contact method
  • A short summary of the problem
  • Appointment dates and provider names
  • For bills: account number, date of service, and the page photo if you are comfortable

Be cautious with

  • Full medical records
  • Photos of IDs and sensitive documents
  • Anything you do not want stored in a volunteer note

If you are unsure, ask us what is needed and we will keep it minimal.

Consent

By contacting us or submitting the form, you are asking to be contacted back. Volunteers may keep brief notes to help with follow up.

Safety and disclaimer
In an emergency, call 911. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988. This site provides general information and free navigation support. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
