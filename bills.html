BILLS AND PAPERWORK

Medical bills can be confusing. We help you identify the next step.

We provide free community-based navigation support. We can help you figure out who to call, what to ask, and what documents to gather. Navigation support only (not legal advice). Not medical advice. Not for emergencies.

Step 1

Identify what the document is
  • Bill vs EOB: an Explanation of Benefits (EOB) is not always a bill.
  • Look for the date of service, account number, and provider name.
  • Check whether insurance processed the claim and what they say you owe.

Step 2

Ask about financial assistance

Hospitals and large systems often have “financial assistance” or “charity care.”

NYC Health + Hospitals financial assistance

Eligibility varies—confirm with the billing office.
