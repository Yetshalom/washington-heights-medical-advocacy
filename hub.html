Mental health

Call/Text 988 NYC Well: 1-888-692-9355 Text WELL to 65173
Fast options (free)
  • 988: crisis support 24/7 (call/text/chat)
  • NYC Well / NYC 988: free counseling support + referrals; interpreters for 200+ languages
Ongoing therapy / psychiatry
  • Community health centers often offer behavioral health (therapy/psychiatry) as part of primary care
  • NYC Health + Hospitals offers outpatient mental health services citywide
  • We can help you find a clinic and schedule

Transportation

I need help getting to appointments

Request Help
What we can do (free)
  • Find subway/bus directions
  • Help with Access-A-Ride steps (paratransit)
  • If eligible, help you ask about Medicaid non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT)
  • Help you ask a clinic/hospital social worker about ride options

We do not personally transport patients.

Medication costs

My prescriptions are too expensive

Request Help
How we help lower costs (free)
  • Compare pharmacy prices using discount tools (like GoodRx / SingleCare)
  • Ask prescriber/pharmacy about generics and cheaper alternatives
  • Look up manufacturer assistance programs when available
  • For seniors: help you explore Medicare Part D / “Extra Help” pathways

We don’t sell insurance and we don’t take payments.
