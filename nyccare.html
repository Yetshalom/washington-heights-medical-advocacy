NYC Care (Low / No-Cost Care)

Emergency? Call 911.

What is NYC Care?

NYC Care is a health care access program through NYC Health + Hospitals for New Yorkers who cannot afford or do not qualify for health insurance.

How to enroll

Call NYC Care: 1-646-NYC-CARE (646-692-2273)

Email: NYCCare@nychhc.org

NYC Care Website Request Help Enrolling

Need help paying a hospital bill or applying for financial assistance?

NYC Health + Hospitals financial counselors can help you apply for insurance coverage or financial assistance.

Call: 1-844-692-4692 (financial counselor option is often listed as option 3).
